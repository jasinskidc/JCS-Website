Profile system with tier access:
	-admin -student -guardian

overall the system will simplify the collection and display of a student's information going from simple thins like name family and birthday to more specific information like current curriculum and submitted grades.
	

For students its a way to create a social environment and allows them to follow their progress

for parents it allows a direct communication with admins and helps keep a detailed track of their child's progress

for admins its gives a detailed breakdown of each student, their progress, and any other information tied to students and their family. connects to the calendar app and allows for a simple way to add students and send them, and parents,
	alerts and other messages regarding congrats for birthdays.
